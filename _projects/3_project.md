**Estimating the Market Response to the Introduction of Plant-Based Meat Substitutes Using Foot Traffic Data**

**Authors:** Erya Ouyang, Y. Wang, X. Luo

**Status:** Manuscript in Preparation

**Abstract:**

This project uses large-scale foot traffic data to estimate consumer demand responses to the introduction of plant-based meat substitutes in retail settings. We apply causal inference methods to identify how the entry of these products affects store visits, purchase patterns, and competition with traditional meat products.

**Keywords:** Retail Analytics, Plant-Based Meat, Foot Traffic Data, Market Response, Causal Inference

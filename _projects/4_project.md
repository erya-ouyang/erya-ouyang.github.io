**Joint Effort or Solo Flight: Strategic Insights into Social Media Posting from a Multimodal Media Design Perspective**

**Authors:** Erya Ouyang, X. Luo

**Status:** Manuscript in Preparation

**Abstract:**

This project investigates the strategic design of social media content, comparing the effectiveness of collaborative versus solo posting strategies. Using a multimodal framework that analyzes visual, audio, and textual components of social media posts, we derive insights for creators and brands seeking to optimize their content for engagement and reach.

**Keywords:** Social Media Strategy, Multimodal Media Design, Content Creation, Influencer Marketing, Digital Platforms

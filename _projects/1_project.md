**Transforming Digital Attention into Social Action: A Multimodal AI Framework for Digital Philanthropy**

**Authors:** Erya Ouyang, J. Ai

**Status:** Data Analysis

**Abstract:**

This project develops a multimodal AI framework to study how digital attention on social media translates into charitable giving and social action. By integrating visual, textual, and engagement signals from nonprofit fundraising campaigns, we aim to identify the mechanisms through which online content drives philanthropic behavior.

**Keywords:** Digital Philanthropy, Multimodal AI, Fundraising, Social Media, Nonprofit Organizations

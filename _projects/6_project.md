**From Exploration to Connection: The Role of Community Ties in Content Creation**

**Author:** Erya Ouyang

**Status:** Data Collection

**Abstract:**

This project explores how community ties and social connections shape content creation behavior on digital platforms. We examine whether creators embedded in stronger communities produce different types of content, achieve higher engagement, and exhibit different career trajectories compared to more isolated creators.

**Keywords:** Community Ties, Content Creation, Social Networks, Digital Platforms, User-Generated Content
